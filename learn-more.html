üîÑ How PTO Sharing Works

PTO Buddy makes it simple for employees to support each other during challenging times. Whether someone is facing a medical emergency, caring for a family member, or dealing with an unexpected crisis, colleagues can donate their unused paid time off hours.

1

Sign Up

Create your account and indicate whether you'd like to donate PTO, request support, or both. Enter your available hours if you're a donor.

2

Browse or Request

View support requests from colleagues or submit your own request explaining your situation and how many hours you need.

3

Donate Hours

Choose how many hours to donate to a colleague in need. Add a supportive message to brighten their day.

4

Track Impact

See your donation history and the collective impact of your organization's generosity.
